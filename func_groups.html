Alcohol

R-OH group. Polar and can form hydrogen bonds.

Carboxylic Acid

Contains both carbonyl and hydroxyl groups: -COOH

Aldehyde

Carbonyl group at the end of a carbon chain: R-CHO

Ketone

Carbonyl group bonded to two carbons: R-CO-R'

Amine

Derived from ammonia: R-NH2, R2NH, or R3N
